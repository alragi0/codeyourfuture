üçé Apple:

Apples are a great source of fiber, which aids digestion and supports gut health. They are low in calories and high in antioxidants, them a healthy snack. Eating apples regularly may reduce the risk of heart disease and type 2 diabetes. They also help with weight control by making you feel full longer. Apples promote oral health and naturally clean your teeth as you chew.

üçä Orange:

Oranges are rich in vitamin C, which helps boost the immune system and fight off infections. They also contain antioxidants that protect cells from damage and reduce inflammation. Oranges are good for heart health as they help lower blood pressure and cholesterol. Drinking fresh orange juice can also improve skin health and hydration. They are a refreshing and hydrating snack,especially in hot weather.

üçå Banana:

Bananas are rich in potassium, which helps regulate blood pressure and support heart health. They provide quick energy, making them ideal for athletes and busy individuals. Bananas also support digestive health thanks to their fiber content. They contain vitamin B6, which helps in brain development and mood regulation. This fruit is also gentle on the stomach and great for preventing cramps.

üçì Strawberry:

Strawberries are packed with vitamin C, fiber, and powerful antioxidants. They support immune health, skin health, and may even help prevent cancer. The antioxidants in strawberries also promote heart health and reduce inflammation. They are low in sugar and calories, making them perfect for healthy diets. Fresh or blended, strawberries are a delicious way to stay healthy.

ü•≠ Mango:

Mangoes are rich in vitamin A, which is essential for good vision and healthy skin. They also contain vitamin C and folate, supporting the immune system and cell growth. Mangoes aid digestion with enzymes like amylase that break down complex carbs. Their antioxidants may help protect against certain cancers and improve brain function. With their sweet taste and nutrients, mangoes are both healthy and satisfying.
